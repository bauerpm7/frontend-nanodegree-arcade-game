Welcome to Udacity Frogger!

Rules

  • To move your character use the arrow keys.
  • You have 3 lives. Lose a life if you are hit by a bug.
  • Score 10 points for making it across the road.
  • Score 20 points for collecting stars.
  • Score 200 points to win the game.
